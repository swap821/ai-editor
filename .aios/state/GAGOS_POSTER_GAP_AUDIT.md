# GAGOS Poster-Fidelity Roadmap — Honest Synthesis (2026-06-22)

## 1. Honest verdict

**Overall perceived poster-fidelity: ~57%** (weighted; simple mean also 57.5%). Weighting biases toward the aspects that carry the poster's emotional and headline reads — Awakening/Conversation, Materialization, Working+Showing-Work, and Cinematic look were each weighted 1.3; Orchestration and Performance 1.2; Motion 1.1; Status 0.9 (it is an operator-approved hybrid, so a chrome deviation should not drag the number); the three "moment" phases (Arrival, Rest, Reabsorption) at 1.0. This matches where a viewer's eye actually spends time: a poster sells on the conversation/materialization/work loop and the film grade, not on the boot animation.

**The truth, calibrated:** GAGOS is an honestly-built, well-tested *system* whose **architecture/skeleton is ~80%** — one fused point cloud, one shared-uniform spine, a real lifecycle FSM, data-true posture colors, a vertebra-seat materialization model, a three-layer reabsorption pipeline, and a sophisticated correctly-ordered AgX film grade all genuinely exist and are unit-tested. But its **perceived visual-fidelity is ~55%**, and the gap is almost entirely *wiring, not invention*: a striking amount of the poster's signature behavior is **already authored and then gated off, dead, or pointed at the wrong target**. The reply-up-the-spine packet, the cortex brighten-while-speaking, the cortex-rooted materialization nerve, the conductor overlay, the reabsorption anchor, the depth haze, the asymmetric breath, and the auto-degrade relief valve are all *code that exists and does not run on the live points home*. The operator's ~55–60% self-estimate is correct and slightly generous. The encouraging implication: closing 15–20 points of perceived fidelity is mostly un-gating, re-pointing, and threading one state — not new shader research.

## 3. The real gap, themed

**Theme A — "Authored but not running on the home" (the gating tax).** The single largest finding across the audit: poster behavior that *exists in code* but is dead, gated to mesh-only, or mis-targeted in the live points being. Reply-up-spine, cortex-brighten-while-speaking, cortex-rooted nerve, the AnatomicalConductorOverlay + AttentionConductionPulse, the reabsorption cortex anchor, depth-haze `uFogDensity`, the asymmetric `uBreath`, the crown-raise, and CURSOR_ATTENTION lean-in are all in this bucket. **Prize: this is where 60% of the recoverable perceived-fidelity lives, at S/M effort and near-zero GPU cost** — un-gating and re-pointing, not new R&D.

**Theme B — Two confirmed *backwards* behaviors that read as bugs, not low fidelity.** (1) The cortex **dims** during a streamed reply (the exact opposite of the poster's "brightens as it speaks"); (2) reabsorption motes converge to a fixed point that **drifts off the cortex** every frame the being voyages/scales (which is always). These are not "missing polish" — they actively contradict the poster. **Prize: outsized; fixing each flips the most-watched moment from wrong to signature.**

**Theme C — The work tab is a viewer, not a workbench.** Phase 6 renders flat monochrome truncated code text with no charts, no syntax color, no in-tab tool telemetry, and a cosmetic 90ms typewriter over already-complete text (the backend emits code as one artifact, so true code-streaming is *not* a cheap win). The poster's hero Phase-6 image is a tab showing **live updating data-viz**. **Prize: the single biggest individual-aspect gap (50%); a real in-tab chart bound to the already-plumbed `metricsStore`/`publishStep` is the highest headline win.**

**Theme D — Cinematic recession + the density-vs-bloom tension.** No depth haze on the being, no volumetric atmosphere, emission bypasses AgX so hot cores clip flat-white, and `uGlowMul` was crushed to kill the white-haze — trading the poster's *lush* glow for *crisp* color. **Prize: the frame currently reads "render preview," not "photographed organism"; depth haze + per-particle filmic rolloff + concentrated-core bloom recover the lush read, all luminance-only/sacred-safe.**

**Theme E — The unenforced 60fps promise + the cohesion clock.** Performance has the full degrade machinery but it's *advisory-only* and `perfTier` is pinned inert, with no GPU probe — so a 3050 (where 256k already breaks) boots worst-case and never recovers; and the points being breathes on a flat sine divorced from the shared organism rhythm. **Prize: the named "must run smoothly" guarantee currently does not exist; resolving FIDELITY-SACRED-vs-smooth by letting *only DPR+post-FX* breathe (never geometry/hue) makes the guarantee real at zero look-cost.**
